# Facial Recognition Authentication

Enable facial recognition to log in securely without typing your password every time!

## What is Facial Recognition Login?

Facial recognition authentication allows you to log in using your face instead of a password. It's:
- ✅ **Secure** - Uses advanced biometric verification
- ✅ **Convenient** - No need to remember passwords
- ✅ **Fast** - Login in seconds
- ✅ **Privacy-focused** - Your facial data is encrypted

## Requirements

Before you start, ensure you have:
- A device with a camera (webcam, laptop camera, or phone camera)
- Good lighting in your environment
- Permission to use your camera and microphone
- An active account on the platform

## Enabling Facial Authentication

### Step 1: Login with Your Password

First, login using your traditional email and password.

### Step 2: Navigate to Profile Settings

1. Click your profile picture in the top right
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Click on the "Security" tab

### Step 3: Enable Facial Recognition

1. Find the "Facial Authentication" section
2. Click "Enable Facial Recognition"
3. Read and accept the privacy notice
4. Click "Continue"

### Step 4: Complete Verification

You'll go through a 3-step wizard:

#### **Step 1: Audio Check** 🔊
- Grant microphone permission when prompted
- This ensures audio works during verification
- Click "Enable Audio"

#### **Step 2: Camera Setup** 📷
- Grant camera permission when prompted
- Position your face in the center of the frame
- Ensure good lighting
- Click "Enable Camera"

#### **Step 3: Verification** ✅
- Watch the instruction video
- Your camera will record you watching
- This creates your facial template
- Takes about 10 seconds
- Click "Start Verification"

### Step 5: Wait for Processing

The system will:
1. Upload your recording (encrypted)
2. Process your facial features
3. Create a secure template
4. Verify the match

This usually takes 3-5 seconds.

### Step 6: Confirmation

Once verified:
- ✅ You'll see a success message
- ✅ Facial authentication is now enabled
- ✅ You can use it for future logins

## Using Facial Recognition to Login

### On the Login Page

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Click "Sign in with Facial Recognition"
3. Enter your email address
4. Click "Start Facial Verification"

### Verification Process

1. **Grant Permissions**: Allow camera and microphone access
2. **Position Yourself**: Face the camera directly
3. **Watch Video**: Follow the on-screen instructions
4. **Wait for Verification**: Processing takes 3-5 seconds
5. **Auto-Login**: You'll be logged in automatically

## Tips for Best Results

### Good Lighting
- Use natural light or good indoor lighting
- Avoid backlighting (window behind you)
- Face should be clearly visible

### Camera Position
- Camera at eye level
- Face centered in frame
- 1-2 feet away from camera

### Environment
- Quiet space (for audio verification)
- Stable internet connection
- No obstructions (glasses are ok, masks are not)

## Troubleshooting

### "Camera not detected"
**Solution**: 
- Check browser permissions
- Ensure camera is not in use by another app
- Try a different browser

### "Verification failed"
**Possible causes**:
- Poor lighting
- Face not clearly visible
- Wrong angle
- Network issues

**Solution**:
- Improve lighting
- Reposition camera
- Try again

### "Session expired"
**Solution**:
- Verification sessions expire after 15 minutes
- Start a new verification

### Still having issues?
- See [Facial Auth Troubleshooting](/docs/user-guide/troubleshooting/facial-auth-issues)
- Contact your estate administrator
- Try traditional login

## Security & Privacy

### Your Data is Protected

- **Encrypted Storage**: All facial data is encrypted at rest
- **Secure Transmission**: Data sent over HTTPS/TLS
- **No Sharing**: Never shared with third parties
- **Your Control**: Disable anytime from settings

### Data Retention

- Facial templates stored for authentication only
- You can delete your facial data anytime
- Auto-deleted if you disable facial auth

### Privacy Compliance

- GDPR compliant
- CCPA compliant
- Full transparency in data usage
- User consent required

## Disabling Facial Authentication

To disable:

1. Go to Profile Settings → Security
2. Find "Facial Authentication"
3. Click "Disable Facial Recognition"
4. Confirm your choice
5. Your facial data will be permanently deleted

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is facial recognition secure?**
A: Yes! It uses the same technology as banking apps and government systems. Your facial data is encrypted and never leaves our secure servers.

**Q: What if someone tries to use my photo?**
A: Our system includes liveness detection to prevent photo/video spoofing. It requires live video, not static images.

**Q: Can I use facial recognition on multiple devices?**
A: Yes! Once enabled, it works on any device with a camera.

**Q: What happens if facial recognition fails?**
A: You can always use your password as a backup.

**Q: Will it work with glasses?**
A: Yes! Glasses are fine. However, sunglasses may cause issues.
