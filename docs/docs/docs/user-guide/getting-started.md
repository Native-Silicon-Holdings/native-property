# Getting Started

Welcome to the Estate Management Platform! This guide will help you get started quickly.

## What You'll Need

- A registered account (provided by your estate administrator)
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Internet connection
- Optional: A device with a camera for facial recognition login

## First Time Setup

### 1. Receive Your Invitation

Your estate administrator will send you an invitation email containing:
- Your username/email
- Temporary password
- Link to the platform

### 2. Access the Platform

Click the link in your invitation email or navigate to:
```
https://your-estate-portal.com
```

### 3. First Login

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter the temporary password
3. Click "Sign in"
4. You'll be prompted to change your password

### 4. Complete Your Profile

After first login:
1. Navigate to your profile (top right corner)
2. Update your personal information
3. Add a phone number (optional but recommended)
4. Upload a profile photo (optional)

## Understanding Your Role

The platform has 5 user roles with different permissions:

| Role | Description | Key Permissions |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Director** | Estate board member | Full access, manage users, approve documents |
| **Manager** | Estate manager | Manage operations, create announcements, handle maintenance |
| **Homeowner** | Property owner | View documents, submit maintenance requests, vote in meetings |
| **Tenant** | Property tenant | Limited access to documents and maintenance requests |
| **Accountant** | Financial officer | Access to financial records and utility billing |

**See your role** in the top navigation bar.

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

### Quick Stats
- Unread announcements
- Pending payments
- Upcoming meetings
- Open maintenance requests

### Recent Activity
- Latest announcements
- Recent documents
- Payment history

### Quick Actions
Depending on your role:
- Submit maintenance request
- View utility bills
- RSVP to meetings
- Upload documents

## Navigation

The platform uses a sidebar navigation:

- 🏠 **Dashboard** - Your home page
- 📄 **Documents** - Estate documents and files
- 📢 **Announcements** - Important updates
- ⚡ **Utilities** - Billing and consumption
- 📅 **Meetings** - AGMs and board meetings
- 🔧 **Maintenance** - Request and track repairs
- 🏘️ **Properties** - Property information
- 👥 **Users** - User management (admin only)
